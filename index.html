What LOEW gives you
LOEW reads power, heart-rate, sleep and recovery metrics to keep your training effective, focused and safe – without needing a human coach.
  • Personalized guidance after every ride.
  • Clear view of load vs. recovery, in simple language.
  • Execution feedback so you know if you hit the plan.
  • Suggestions when to push, hold, or rest.
AI-powered Cycling-first Recovery-aware
How LOEW helps you
LOEW gives you clarity and direction for each day of training, using your real data instead of guesswork or generic plans.
  • Built for cyclists and endurance riders.
  • Works whether you chase PRs or just want to ride safely.
  • Simple daily decisions: ride, go easy, or rest.
Connect your devices
LOEW connects to platforms like Garmin and Strava to use the data you already collect on every ride.
  • Analyzes power, heart-rate, GPS and elevation.
  • Combines training load with sleep and recovery metrics (when available).
  • Gives timely feedback right after your sessions.
Privacy & data protection
Your health and activity data are sensitive. LOEW is built with privacy and user control as core principles.
  • Explicit consent for every connection (Garmin, Strava, etc.).
  • Secure storage, encrypted in transit, limited access.
  • No selling of identifiable data to third parties.
  • Easy disconnect and data deletion on request.
