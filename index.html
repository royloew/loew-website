Health-first endurance coaching

A smarter way back to the bike after cancer.

LOEW is a personalized recovery & cycling coach built by a leukemia survivor, helping patients and survivors return safely to endurance training using real data from Garmin, Strava and other platforms.

לואו (LOEW) הוא מאמן חכם לחזרה בטוחה לאימונים אחרי מחלה קשה, עם ניתוח נתונים מהשעון והמחשב רכיבה כדי להגן על הבריאות ולתכנן עומס נכון.

What LOEW focuses on
LOEW uses power, heart-rate, GPS, elevation, sleep and recovery metrics to keep training effective but safe for athletes coming back from serious illness.
  • Monitoring load vs. recovery for cancer survivors.
  • Estimating FTP and HR thresholds conservatively.
  • Adaptive training plans based on recent data.
  • Flagging unusual responses and suggesting rest.
Cycling-first Recovery-oriented Evidence-inspired
The LOEW concept
LOEW is not a social network. It is a focused tool to help people rebuild fitness after serious illness, using clear metrics and simple coaching language rather than complex dashboards.
  • Built from a survivor's perspective.
  • Designed for cyclists, runners and walkers.
  • Simple, daily decisions: ride, rest, or adjust.
Why Garmin Connect access matters
Garmin devices already capture the depth of data we rely on: detailed FIT files, heart-rate, power, sleep, stress and body-battery. Direct access allows LOEW to give timely and safe recommendations.
  • Near real-time feedback after every session.
  • Accurate FTP and HR analysis from full data.
  • Combining training load with daily wellness metrics.
Privacy & data protection
Health and activity data are treated as highly sensitive. LOEW is committed to strict privacy and control for each user.
  • Explicit consent for every connection (Garmin, Strava, etc.).
  • Secure storage, encrypted in transit, limited access.
  • No selling of identifiable data to third parties.
  • Easy disconnect and data deletion on request.
