What LOEW does

LOEW analyzes real-world cycling and activity data (power, heart rate, GPS, elevation, sleep, recovery metrics) from connected wearables to build a safe, adaptive training plan for individuals returning to sport after serious illness.

The platform focuses on:

  • Monitoring load vs. recovery for cancer survivors and patients.
  • Estimating FTP, heart-rate thresholds and intensity zones safely.
  • Recommending daily and weekly training tailored to medical history.
  • Flagging abnormal responses (HR, power, fatigue) and suggesting rest.

Why we need Garmin Connect access

Garmin devices already capture the depth of data we need: power, heart rate, sleep, stress, body battery and detailed FIT files . Direct access to Garmin Connect allows LOEW to:

  • Deliver near real-time feedback after each session.
  • Use full-fidelity FIT/GPX data for accurate power & HR analysis.
  • Combine daily health metrics with training load for safer decisions.

We expect an initial beta of ~50–100 users (survivors & high-risk athletes), with modest API usage per user per week and strict adherence to Garmin’s developer and brand guidelines.

Privacy & data protection

LOEW treats health and activity data as highly sensitive. We will:

  • Request explicit consent for every data connection (Garmin, Strava, etc.).
  • Store data securely with access controls and encryption in transit.
  • Never sell or share identifiable data with third parties.
  • Allow users to disconnect and delete their data at any time.

A full privacy policy and data-processing agreement will be provided as part of any formal integration or partnership.
